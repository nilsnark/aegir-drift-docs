## Fold terms

**Foldstone**  
A small realspace artifact anchoring a vast pocket dimension.

**Fold Kernel**  
A specialized Foldstone containing a massive computational architecture used to regulate Foldspace systems.

**NPF (Negative Potential Flux)**  
The exotic energy substrate enabling Foldstones, Kernels, and wormhole engineering.

**SLISP**  
A safe, substrate-level Lisp dialect used for scripting devices, automation, drones, and Foldstone systems.

**Seedship**  
A micro-probe Foldstone craft used to deploy wormhole anchors across interstellar distances.

**Gate / Wormhole**
